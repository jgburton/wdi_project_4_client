REMOTE COMPOSER

An open source composition platform, for composers who want to colaborate.

James is a composer, he uploads an 'idea'. (e.g Piano Motif - 30 seconds)

Another composer Lauren listens to James idea and feels inspired.

Lauren adds an 'addition' (another single instrument track - e.g violin) to the 'idea'.

The process continues resulting in a beautiful and collabrative composition.
